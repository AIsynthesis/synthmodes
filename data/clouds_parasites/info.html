Clouds Parasites

Clouds is a multi-function granular audio processor by Mutable Instruments.

Clouds Parasites is a free alternate firmware for Clouds by Matthias Puech that adds functionality to existing modes, as well as adding dedicated reverb and resonator modes.

The layout of these cheat sheets are inspired by the original cheat sheet by Erstlaub.

To change modes, hold the "blend parameter" button for 5 seconds until the LEDs turn orange, then tap the blend button again to change modes.
