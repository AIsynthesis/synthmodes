Pattern (Bank)
Press View/change active pattern.
Shift + Press View/change active pattern bank.

Run/Stop (Rec)
Press Start/stop sequencer.
Shift + Press Begin recording when sequencer is in keyboard mode.
Shift + Press (Held) Hold for >1sec to begin saving pattern.

Reset/Accent
Press When sequencer running, sets pattern back to step 1.
Step Edit Toggle step's accent output.

Hold/Rest
Press When sequencer running, holds sequence at current step while button held.
Step Edit Sets/unsets active step as a rest.

Tempo / Gate Length (swing)
TURN Adjust sequencer tempo.
Step edit Controls percentage of step that gate will remain high.
Shift + Turn Adjusts sequencer swing

Glide (Ratchet)
TURN Adjust oscillator glide
Shift+Turn (Step edit) Sets number of gates (1-4) that will occur during currently edited step.

(KB)
Press (step edit) Decrements active step during step editing.
Shift Press Sets sequencer mode to keyboard.

(Step)
Press (step edit) Increments active step during step editing.
Shift Press Sets sequencer mode to step editing.
