Black Hole DSP 2

Black Hole DSP 2 is a multi effect module with 24 algorithms based on Spin FV-1 chip

Saving / Recalling Settings

To save effect settings hold down the "Patch" encoder until the screen indicates "SA". To recall saved settings double click the "Patch" encoder.

To edit a parameter after recall move the knob until the red dot on the screen blinks once. This indicates you have reached the saved position.
