Disting mk3

Disting mk3 is a eurorack swift army knife. It is made by Expert Sleepers.

The Disting firmware gets updated regularly, be sure to check the firmware page for the latest version and latest manual.

These cheat sheets cover firmware V3.10.

Pressing the S encoder once enters the menu for switching algorithms and banks. Select the first item menu to change algorithms. Select the second menu item to change banks. Press Z to cancel and exit.

For additional details about parameters, scales, and note numbers see the excellent cheatshhet by vo1t.
